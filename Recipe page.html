Chocolate Cake Recipe

This is a simple recipe for making a chocolate cake.

Image

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, and baking powder.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, and butter.
  4. Mix everything until smooth.
  5. Pour the mixture into a baking pan.
  6. Bake for 35 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool before serving.

Tips

  • Use fresh ingredients.
  • Do not open the oven while baking.
  • Serve with chocolate sauce or ice cream.
